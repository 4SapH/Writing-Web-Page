	Absenta in cer
	Aer. Inspira lent si precaut.
	Expira grabit si insetat.
	Se ineaca neincetat,
	se lasa usor anesteziat.
	Ametit, ii place sa viseze
	nu se opreste sa asculte;
	prefera sa creeze
	cu desfranata sa minte.

	Ascunde
	in miezul fiintei sale
	secretul absentei
	ratiunii sale;
	conceptul enigmatic al
	intregei sale vremelnitati,
	felul in care nebunia sa
	se lasa purtata de val.
	Prefere sa se inece inotand in vazduh
	decat sa zboare prin timp.
				




	A sky and no moon
	to light its heart,
	a heart and no mind
	to concretize its hope,
	a hope and no place
	to keep it in.	
				




	He opened the door and saw it. Death came in.

	The mirror framed the killer.
				




	He left the package there. She came in and started crying. He couldn’t thank him or save him.

	What she found wasn’t his heart, nor his mind; it was his body,
	hanging.
